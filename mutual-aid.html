🌼 Tend to the Mutual Aid Scrolls

This space honors the commitments we make to one another—not through transaction, but through trust.

🧭 What Is Mutual Aid?

Mutual aid is the practice of showing up for each other. It’s not charity. It’s solidarity. It’s the architecture of care, built from shared needs and shared offerings.

“We affirm that support is not earned—it is offered. We affirm that visibility is not requested—it is granted.”

🌿 How We Tend the Scrolls

  • We document needs and offerings with clarity and care
  • We honor pauses, and respond without urgency
  • We treat every update as a lantern lit for the group
  • We hold space for emotional check-ins alongside technical flows
