Positive Community Impact

Utility Rating:

  • 1: Low utility (e.g., decorative items, non-essential) - 2 points.
  • 2: Moderate utility (e.g., slightly useful, but not impactful) - 4 points.
  • 3: Average utility (e.g., useful for daily tasks) - 6 points.
  • 4: High utility (e.g., important for well-being or productivity) - 8 points.
  • 5: Essential utility (e.g., critical supplies, life-enhancing) - 10 points.
Effort Rating:
  • 1: Minimal effort (e.g., easily available, inexpensive) - Multiplier: x1.
  • 2: Low effort (e.g., requires some planning or cost) - Multiplier: x2.
  • 3: Moderate effort (e.g., requires time, moderate cost) - Multiplier: x3.
  • 4: High effort (e.g., significant time or cost) - Multiplier: x4.
  • 5: Exceptional effort (e.g., rare, expensive, or time-intensive) - Multiplier: x5.
Point Calculation: Utility points × Effort multiplier × 2.
Time Shared: Hours × 5 (e.g., 1 hour = 5 points).
Money Donated: Amount ÷ 10 (e.g., $10 = 1 point).
